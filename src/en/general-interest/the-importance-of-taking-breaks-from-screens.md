Spending too much time in front of devices can affect sleep and concentration. Taking short breaks during the day helps restore energy and mental clarity.

Simple activities like walking, cooking, or talking without a phone nearby can improve mood.

Disconnecting for a while also helps people enjoy their surroundings and relationships more fully.
